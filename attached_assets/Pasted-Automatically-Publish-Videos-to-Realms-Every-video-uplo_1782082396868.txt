Automatically Publish Videos to Realms
Every video uploaded anywhere in the application should automatically appear in the Realms section.
Users should not need to create a separate Realm after uploading a video.

Videos posted from:

Home Feed
Communities
User Profiles
Bible Study Groups
Church Communities

should automatically be indexed and displayed in Realms.

Realms should become the central video discovery experience across the platform.
Videos should appear in:
User's Realms tab
Followers' feeds
Explore/Search
Community feeds (where applicable)
Realms feed
Allow users to optionally disable "Show in Realms" before publishing.
